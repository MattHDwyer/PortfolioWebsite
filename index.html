ABOUT ME

I'm a developer who enjoys working on the back-end development of projects!

Approaching everything I undertake with an optimistic outlook, I look forward to designing and coding challenging and useful projects that greatly benefit the end user. I have a defined sense for how things should work and I'm always looking to take on other peoples advice and opinions on how I should go about my work.

I’ve built applications using Ruby, HTML/CSS. I’m currently undertaking Coder Academy’s GenTech bootcamp course, where by the end of the course I’ll have knowledge in Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, MongoDB, Node and React, as well as small amounts of knowledge in: Express, SQL and Postgres.

When I’m not coding or working spending time with my family, going out with my mates, gaming as well as doing some fun coding challenges.
